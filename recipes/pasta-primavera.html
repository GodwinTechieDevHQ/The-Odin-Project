Pasta Primavera

Description

Pasta Primavera is a fresh and vibrant dish made with pasta, a variety of colorful vegetables, and a light garlic and olive oil sauce. It's a perfect dish for a light and healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables and sauté until tender.
  4. Mix the cooked pasta with the vegetables, season with salt and pepper, and top with Parmesan cheese if desired.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
