Chocolate Cake

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is the ultimate dessert for chocolate lovers. With layers of fluffy cake and a decadent chocolate frosting, it’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix together the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder) in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the wet ingredients (oil, eggs, vanilla, milk) and combine with the dry ingredients.
  4. Gradually add boiling water and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool before frosting. Enjoy!
