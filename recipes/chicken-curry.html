Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry is a popular dish in many cultures, made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a flavorful, aromatic curry sauce. This version is spiced to perfection and is perfect when served with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken (boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in curry powder, tomato sauce, and coconut milk. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  4. Season with salt and garnish with cilantro.
  5. Serve with rice or naan and enjoy!
